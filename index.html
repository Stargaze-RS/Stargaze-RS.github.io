International Content Sharing Platform

At Walmart International, I orchestrated a comprehensive Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) platform for internal-facing sites serving 7+ markets. Due to corporate confidentiality, I cannot display direct site screenshots, so I've prepared detailed wireframes and documentation that illustrate my design approach. By developing standardized website templates and implementing rigorous quality assurance processes, we created a unified digital ecosystem. The platform enhanced content management efficiency, enabled localized internal experiences, and streamlined website updates across global teams. Our strategic approach improved digital consistency and operational efficiency.
